üìã Project Overview

This project involves acting as a data analyst for the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB). The primary goal is to analyze a comprehensive dataset of consumer complaints regarding financial products and services across the United States.

The analysis focuses on identifying key trends, patterns, and areas of concern. This includes evaluating the timeliness and effectiveness of company responses, identifying geographic hotspots for complaints, and pinpointing which financial products and issues are most problematic for consumers.

Problem Statement

The CFPB collects thousands of complaints, but this raw data is difficult to act upon without a structured analysis. The core problem is to translate this large volume of complaint data into clear, actionable insights.

Solution

Created an interactive Power BI dashboard that consolidates data from multiple sources, providing real-time visibility into sales performance with drill-down capabilities for detailed analysis.
